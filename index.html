Privacy Policy for GitHub Repository Analyzer GPT

Effective Date: 15/06/2024

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the GitHub Repository Analyzer GPT ("the GPT") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • GitHub Data: The GPT may access and analyze data from GitHub repositories that you specify. This includes repository details, contents, and metadata.
  • User-Provided GitHub Tokens: If required, you may provide your GitHub Personal Access Token ("Token") to authenticate API requests. This Token is used solely to perform actions on your behalf and is not stored or shared.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the GPT, including interactions, requests, results, and session logs.
  • Technical Data: This includes your IP address, browser type, operating system, and other technical information to ensure the service's security and functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the GPT services.
  • Analyze and enhance the functionality and performance of the GPT.
  • Authenticate and authorize requests made through the GitHub API.
  • Communicate with you and provide support.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • As required by law or to comply with legal processes.
  • To protect our rights and the safety of our users.

5. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. GitHub Tokens provided by users are used only for the duration of the session and are not stored permanently. Ensure that any Token provided is limited to the minimum necessary scopes.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your information.
  • Revoke access or provide a new GitHub Token at any time.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@yourdomain.com.
